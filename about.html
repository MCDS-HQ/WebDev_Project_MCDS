ABOUT US

SNOWTRAIL TRAVELS is a company founded by avid hiker and entrepreneur Lucas Snow in 2014 to help his clients in booking suitable travel destinations for their pursuit of winter sports without all the usual hassle caused. The company originally operated through a physical headquarters, but with the ever-growing nature of travel, the company eventually expanded to operating digitally in 2024, through the very website you are using now.
